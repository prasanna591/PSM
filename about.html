Who We Are

The story of PSM

Problem Solving Mind (PSM) was founded in 2026 as a technology-driven company dedicated to solving real-world challenges through innovation, education, and creativity.

What began as a small group of builders and educators has grown into a 50+ person ecosystem spanning software engineering, hands-on education, and creative production — each operating as its own discipline, all aimed at the same outcome.

We don't see ourselves as a service company. We see ourselves as an ecosystem where technology gets built, people get trained to build it themselves, and brands get the creative push to be seen.

Mission

Solving real-world problems through technology, education, and creativity — building products that work, learners who can build, and brands that grow.

Vision

To create an ecosystem where innovation, learning, and digital growth coexist — where every idea has a path to becoming real.
